UI WIDGET- TABS: 
Tabs are the set of logically grouped content that facilitates users to flip between them.
        Tab must be in ordered or unordered list
SYNTAX: 
        $(selector, context).tabs (options) 

        $(selector, context).tabs ("action", params)
OPTIONS: 
        active, disabled,collapsible,event,heightStyle,hide,show
ACTION: 
       destroy,disable,disable(index),enable,enable(index),load(index),option,refresh,widget
